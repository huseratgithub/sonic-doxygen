34 *
35 * Here is to implement orchagent_restart_check binary which may talk to orchagent and
36 * ask it to do self-check, return "READY " signal and freeze if everything is ok,
37 * otherwise "NOT_READY" signal should be returned.

39 * Optionally:
40 * if --noFreeze option is provided, orchagent won't freeze.
